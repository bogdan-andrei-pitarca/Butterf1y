About Us

At Butterf1y, we understand how difficult it can be to find people who share your interests.

The Butterf1y team is a group of five passionate individuals united by a shared vision: to bring people together and help them feel a sense of belonging within a supportive community.



Our Mission

The Butterf1y team is dedicated to supporting individuals who experience social anxiety by organizing events specifically designed to bring together people with shared interests. Our goal is to create a friendly and safe environment where participants can interact naturally and comfortably.

At each event, attendees will have access to a dedicated guide whose role is to introduce them to new places, engage them in exciting activities, and foster connections among participants. Our mission is to help individuals overcome social barriers and build meaningful relationships in an enjoyable and relaxed setting.
